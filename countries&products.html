COUNTRIES AND PRODUCTS

Below are the countries wherefrom the sourcing is presently being / can be done through relevant counterparts :-

  • Bangladesh
  • India
  • Indonesia
  • Pakistan
  • Vietnam

Bangladesh:-

True in sense, it can be regarded as the most vibrant and progressive country, though still least developing, but with the help of highly skilled work force, state of the art production set up, and liberal Government policies, it is advancing rapidly and dethroning its major rivals in both South Asia and ASEAN.
Hitherto, 52 countries including Australia, Canada, European Union, Japan, New Zealand, Norway, Russia, Switzerland, Turkey have allowed duty free excess for all kinds of products from Bangladesh except arms and ammunitions. Whereas China 4788 items, Malaysia 297 items, South Korea 8778 items, Thailand 6999 items and the USA 8620 items, have agreed to give the said status from Bangladesh. Brazil is the latest one to do the same but in phases.
Bar Mops, Bath / Bed / Kitchen Linens, Fabrics, Sheeting, Fish and its preparations, are to name a few products that can be offered from Bangladesh.

India :

Historically, India is thought to be the foremost country producing both cotton and agriculture products. 18% of the world’s total cotton production is being done by India and is officially 2nd largest producer. It is also the pioneer among introducers of Better Cotton Initiative, commenced in the year 2014. India has an edge over its competitors.
Plain dyed towels, yarn dyed towels, hotel towels, jacquard towels and a wide range of kitchen linens can be arranged from a large unit based in Mumbai.

Indonesia :-

The world’s largest island country has lots of items to offer. We are striving hard to gain a foothold in the market, already working with a well reputed mill that produces the linen fabric. We anticipate early shipments from there as one of our buyers has shown keen interest in start buying the same due to unmatchable quality and low price tag.

Pakistan :

Pakistan is a developing country and is one of the Next Eleven, the eleven countries that have potential to be among the world’s largest economies in the 21st century. Based here, we are in an excellent position to source as many products as needed by our valued buyers. Starting from the textiles that contribute almost 60% of total revenue to the economy which is the 25th largest in the world in terms of purchasing power parity (PPP) and 42nd largest in terms of gross domestic product (GDP).
Primary export commodities are textiles, leather goods, sports goods, rice, halal meat (chicken, mutton, sheep, cow, camel), sea foods, chemicals, carpets / rugs and medical instruments etc. Apart from these, sugar, wheat are also being done in large volumes but these two products depend on the respective crop sizes.
China, Malaysia and Sri Lanka have free trade agreements, whereas Indonesia, Iran, Mauritius have preferential trade agreements with Pakistan. The European Union has allotted GSP Plus status in its favor.

Vietnam :-

A truly Asian Tiger, since year 2000 Vietnam’s economic growth rate has been among the highest in the world and in 2011 it had the highest Global Growth Generators Index among 11 major economies.
Through our associate based in Ho Chi Minh City, the largest city, can offer Black Pepper, Cashew Nuts, Rice, Tea, Coffee, Plywood, Fishery products (specially world’s renowned Pangasius), Float Glass, Towels, Galvalume, Aluminum ingots etc.
